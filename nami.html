Nami

I created my own brand of instant noodles, trying to modernise the way in which instant noodle packaging happens by getting influenced by Japanese minimalist design aesthetics and line drawing style illustrations. The word 'Nami' itself means 'wave' in Japanese so I was heavily drawing inspiration from that throughout the design process.
