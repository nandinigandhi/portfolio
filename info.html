—Info


Hey! I am nandini. I'm a third-year Graphic Design student at OCAD University in Toronto. I am a highly motivated individual and use my graphic design skills to pay close attention to detail and create intentionality in everything I do. My main goal as a Graphic Designer is to create experiences through my work and focus on visual storytelling.

I'm interested in generative art, web development, concept development, creative programming, visual identity systems, typography, layout design, product photography, user interface design and user experience design. I am eager to acquire new skills and knowledge, all the while infusing my own experiences into diverse environments. My curiosity extends to design tools, reading, design philosophy, vocal music, and color psychology.
