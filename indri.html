Indri

Info Are.na E-Mail
Menu

I wanted to try to create something out of my confort zone and I landed on rebranding the bottle of an Indin Whiskey company named Indri. The word 'Indri' in Sanskrit refers to the five senses: touch, taste, sight, sound, and smell and so I wanted to create a bottle that encopassed all five senses and created a sensory experience.

Here are the tools and software I used to create this piece of work:
— Adobe InDesign
— Adobe Illustrator
— Blender
